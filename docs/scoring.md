# Scoring Note

The assistant ranks jobs on a 100-point rubric tuned for senior PM and Strategy & Operations roles.

## Criteria

- Role fit: seniority and title alignment for Sr. PM, Principal PM, Sr. Manager Strategy & Ops, and Director-level tracks.
- Domain leverage: preference for marketplaces, fintech, operations-heavy SaaS, vendor platforms, and AI tooling where Amazon, Goldman, and GAGA experience compounds.
- Comp and level: full marks when public base pay is at or above $220K; otherwise inferred from title and company stage.
- Company stage: preference for Series B-D and strong public companies; penalties for very early or stagnant late-stage roles.
- Logistics: full marks for NYC and remote roles, partial for SF hybrid, zero for relocation-heavy roles.

## Exclusions

- Contract and contract-to-hire roles
- Titles containing `Associate` or `APM` unless also marked `Senior`
- Postings older than 30 days

## Why this rubric

The goal is not broad job discovery. The goal is to surface roles with high odds of strong fit, compensation, and practical follow-through.
